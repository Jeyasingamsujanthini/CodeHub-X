"Sri Lanka Introduces Comprehensive Tax Reforms to Stimulate Economic Recovery"

2024/05/02

In response to the economic challenges posed by the COVID-19 pandemic and mounting debt obligations, the Sri Lankan government has unveiled a series of sweeping tax reforms aimed at revitalizing the economy. The reforms include measures to simplify the tax code, broaden the tax base, and enhance revenue collection efforts. Key initiatives include the introduction of a progressive income tax system, the expansion of the value-added tax (VAT) regime to cover previously exempted sectors, and the implementation of targeted tax incentives to spur investment and innovation. The government emphasizes the importance of these reforms in promoting fiscal sustainability, attracting foreign investment, and fostering inclusive growth in Sri Lanka.
