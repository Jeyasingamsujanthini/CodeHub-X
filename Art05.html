"Taxation and Gender Equality in Sri Lanka: Bridging the Divide Through Inclusive Fiscal Policies"

2024/05/02

This section introduces the article's focus on the gender dimensions of taxation in Sri Lanka and its implications for gender equality.Unpacking Gendered Impacts of Taxation: Challenges and Realities: Here, the article examines how tax policies affect women differently and perpetuate gender inequalities in access to resources, opportunities, and decisionmaking power.Women's Economic Empowerment: Addressing Taxation's Gender Bias: This part discusses the importance of women's economic empowerment and the role of taxation policies in promoting gender equality and women's rights in Sri Lanka.Reducing Inequalities: Policy Frameworks for Gender-Responsive Taxation: The article explores potential policy frameworks for gender-responsive taxation and advocates for measures that promote women's economic autonomy and empowerment.Advancing Social Justice: Ensuring Gender Equality in Taxation and Beyond: Finally, the article calls for comprehensive strategies to advance gender equality in taxation and beyond, emphasizing the importance of inclusive fiscal policies for building a more just and equitable society.
