"Sri Lanka Grapples with Rising Tax Evasion Amid Economic Turmoil"

2024/05/02

Despite efforts to strengthen tax enforcement mechanisms, Sri Lanka continues to face significant challenges in combating tax evasion, particularly in the context of a prolonged economic downturn exacerbated by the COVID-19 pandemic. Recent investigations by tax authorities have uncovered widespread instances of tax fraud, evasion, and illicit financial flows, depriving the government of much-needed revenue and undermining public confidence in the tax system. In response, the government has pledged to ramp up enforcement efforts, enhance cooperation with international partners to tackle cross-border tax evasion, and implement measures to improve transparency and accountability in tax administration. However, experts warn that addressing systemic weaknesses in tax governance and enforcement will require sustained political will, institutional reforms, and international cooperation.
